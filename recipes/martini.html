Martini

Description

The martini is a coctail made with gin and vermouth, and garnished with an olive or a lemon twist. Over the years, the martini has become one of the best-known mixed alcoholic beverages. A popular variation, the vodka martini, uses vodka instead of gin for the cocktail's base spirit.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 oz (1 part) Dry vermouth
  • 3 oz (6 parts) Gin

Preparation

  1. Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well
  3. Strain in chilled martini cocktail glass
  4. Squeeze oil from lemon peel onto the drink, or garnish with olive

Garnish

Lemon twist, Olive
