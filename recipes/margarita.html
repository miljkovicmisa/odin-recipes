Margarita

Description

A margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, triple sec, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is served shaken with ice, blended with ice, or without ice. The drink is generally served in a stepped-diameter variant of a cocktail glass or champagne coupe called a margarita glass.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 1 oz Lime juice
  • 2 oz Tequila

Preparation

  1. Rub the rim of the glass with the lime slice to maike the salt stick to it.
  2. Shake the other ingredients with ice, then carefully pour into the glass (taking care not to sislodge any salt).
  3. Garnish and serve over ice.

Garnish

Lime slice, Salt on the rim
